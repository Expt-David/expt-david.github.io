For a long period, young researchers on gravity work separately. The high requirement of Mathematics such as differential geometry results in a high threshold on the research of gravity theory like General Relativity. Thus, the capable persons are separated among people. There is nothing bad for us to do our research alone, for innovation nowadays usually comes from a small spark of an individual. Nevertheless, academy needs communication. This is the reason for this blog. The blog aims at providing a platform for young physicists on gravity to communicate and share ideas, so that we can benefit from our participation.

About the Academy

The blog is for professional academic discussion of young physicists on gravity research. And hence, if you are a researcher of other area or you are coming for some public understanding of science content, this might not be a suitable place.
